Next Steps for Implementation

1. Implement Your Plus One Plan

Based on the implementation plan you created, start by focusing on just one enhancement to your teaching. Remember that small, targeted improvements can have significant impact.

  • Review your implementation timeline and gather any necessary resources
  • Start with your identified "pinch point" where learners face the most challenges
  • Document your changes and student responses to inform future improvements

2. Gather Feedback

As you implement your enhancements, collect feedback from your learners to understand the impact of your changes and identify opportunities for further improvement.

  • Ask students about their experience with the enhanced content
  • Compare assessment outcomes before and after implementation
  • Be open to unexpected outcomes and adjust your approach as needed

3. Continue Your UDL Journey

The Plus One approach is about continuous, incremental improvement. After successfully implementing your first enhancement, consider your next area of focus.

  • Look for additional "pinch points" in your instruction
  • Explore new UDL strategies and techniques through the resources below
  • Share your experiences with colleagues to build a community of practice
