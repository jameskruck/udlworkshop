Welcome to the UDL Plus One Workshop

This workshop explores how Universal Design for Learning (UDL) can enhance learning experiences. By the end of this session, you will have a practical plan to improve your teaching using the Plus One Approach - a strategy for implementing UDL principles in your courses.

Before we start planning out how you are going to use the Plus One Approach in your courses, let's take a look at how the approach might help us understand complex topics.

Choose a Topic to Explore

Select one of the two topics below to get started. You'll experience the biggest effect if you choose one that you're not familiar with. You want to be in the seat of a student for this experience.

Music Theory: Modal Interchange

Discover how modal interchange can add emotional depth to music by borrowing chords from parallel modes.

Statistics: Chi-Square Test of Independence

Learn how to determine whether two categorical variables are independent using the chi-square test.
